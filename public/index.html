03 — Approach

Hardware,
interaction,
evidence.

I work end-to-end. Most projects start at a soldering iron and end with a peer-reviewed claim about how people perceive an interaction.

  • 01
    Research & framing

    Literature review, competitive teardown, formative interviews.

  • 02
    Prototyping

    Thermal arrays, ultrasound rigs, gloves, EMG straps + Unity / XR Toolkit interactions.

  • 03
    User testing

    Within-subject studies, IRB protocols, mixed-methods analysis (quant + think-aloud).

  • 04
    Communication

    Design implications, papers, deck-ready visuals for stakeholders.
