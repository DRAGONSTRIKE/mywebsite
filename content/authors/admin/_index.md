## About Me

I design sensory feedback systems that make virtual reality feel physical — thermal gloves, mid-air haptic displays, and cross-modal wearables that ask and answer: *what should touch feel like in XR?*

My work spans hardware fabrication, Unity VR development, and IRB-approved user studies. Eight peer-reviewed papers at **ACM CHI, UIST, IMWUT, IEEE VR, ISMAR**, and **VRST**. 🏆 Best Demo at World Haptics Conference 2025.

Finishing my PhD at UT Dallas and actively looking for roles in **UX Research**, **Product Design**, and **XR Development** — anywhere interaction quality is taken seriously.
